How K-Means Works:

  1. Initialization: Random points are selected as initial cluster centers (centroids)
  2. Assignment: Each data point is assigned to the nearest centroid
  3. Update: Centroids are moved to the mean position of their assigned points
  4. Repeat: Steps 2-3 repeat until convergence
