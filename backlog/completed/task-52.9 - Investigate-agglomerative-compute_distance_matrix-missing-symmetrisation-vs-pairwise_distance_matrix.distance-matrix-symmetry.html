Finding — confirmed and documented
Every off-diagonal entry is written from the same scalar to both D[i·n+j] and D[j·n+i], so D[i,j] ≡ D[j,i] exactly — not approximately. The suspected asymmetry cannot occur. The (D+Dᵀ)/2 step in pairwise_distance_matrix exists only because that path computes the two sides independently; here it would be a no-op. Inline stays; the guarantee is now stated in JSDoc and locked by an unconditional per-metric test.
